About Me

Who I Am

  • My desire to get to know people at a deeper level has been an enduring force in my career.
  • I believe we are all interconnected and what benefits one of us has a ripple effect to many others.
  • My highest value is in promoting compassion and dignity in the hope that those I serve will experience self-compassion, dignity and self-empowerment.

What I Do

I help clients:
  • Identify, with greater self awareness, the problems they are experiencing.
  • Understand what treatment alternatives are available.
  • Determine what course of action they prefer to take.
  • By prescribing medications as indicated.
  • Through counseling, education and providing support to them and their loved ones.

What I Bring

  • Board certification as Clinical Nurse Specialist in Adult Psychiatric and Mental Health, by the ANCC.
  • Licensed by Ohio Chemical Dependency Professionals Board as a Licensed Independent Chemical Dependency Counselor.
  • Worked in community mental health as a clinician and program administrator for the past 18 years.
  • Treated patients and consulted with staff in private community hospitals and public institutions including Veterans Administration.
  • Taught nursing students at Cleveland State University and Ursuline College.
  • Precepted graduate nursing students and medical students attending Ohio universities.
